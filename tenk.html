TEN.K

TEN.K is poem generator that pulls from a bank of phrases using a combination of user input and pseudo-randomness. Designing and curating its contents has been a challenge of grammatical and figurative consistency. Occasionally TEN.K will produce oblique poems of dubious quality; other times they may seem hand-written and personal. I hope to gradually expand and refine this project's lexicon in the future to better embody the promise of "ten thousand poems".
