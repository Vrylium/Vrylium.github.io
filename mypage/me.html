Introduction

Updated: May 30 2022


I'm Katherine and this is my self-portrait; the canvas of my personality, hopes, and ideas. It's not an archive of the past. It's a representation of my present self, whoever that may be. I update according to no predictable schedule, only the schedule that emerges from the rhythm of my life events.


I studied biochemistry in college and became the manager of a food safety quality assurance department and its laboratory in 2021. While my career is focused within the biological and chemical sciences, I'm equally passionate about literature and art.


Generally, I'm quiet and reserved, preferring peaceful and relaxed environments whether found indoors or in nature. In comfortable company, I open up a lot. I don't even self-describe as shy anymore. I like asking people questions and hearing their stories. I'm enthusiastic about pretty much everything that isn't sports-related. If you know where and how to look, the world always has something interesting to offer.
