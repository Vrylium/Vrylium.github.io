ABOUT

The Vrylium Project is the eclectic hobbyist portfolio of Katherine Villa, with a mission of honing preexisting skills and cultivating new ones in an environment of zero monetary pressure. This means all content is free to access! Expect passion projects such as, but not limited to: literature, game design and appreciation, and blog-adjacent oddities.

This site claims association with no external social media accounts. But if you like reading, follow on StoryGraph for book reviews and recommendations.
